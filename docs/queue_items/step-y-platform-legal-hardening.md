## STEP Y — PLATFORM LEGAL HARDENING + TRUST SAFETY PASS

Status: queued (Tier 1 starts immediately after queue insertion)

Objective:
Ship a full legal + UX risk-reduction pass before scale and before any monetization changes.

---

## TIER 1 — Legal foundation (do first, no exceptions)

1. Rewrite Terms of Service with enforceable risk allocation language.
2. Add/strengthen limitation of liability with explicit exposure cap.
3. Standardize platform role language across Terms + footer + page copy as “lead-generation platform”.
4. Add user responsibility + assumption of risk clauses in Terms.
5. Add broad “as-is” / no-warranty clause for the platform.

Exit criteria:
- `/terms` reflects all five requirements
- public copy aligns with Terms language

---

## TIER 2 — Listing-level protection

6. Add visible disclaimer on every listing page: ownership + data accuracy responsibility.
7. Ensure listing status is explicit: verified vs unverified (no ambiguity).
8. Remove/avoid copy that implies transaction facilitation or guarantees.
9. Update inquiry form/support copy to avoid guaranteed outcomes/response implications.

Exit criteria:
- listing template includes disclaimer block
- verification labeling policy is visible in UI

---

## TIER 3 — Data and privacy compliance

10. Publish/refresh Privacy Policy to match actual data flow.
11. Explicitly disclose that inquiry lead data is shared with listing owners.
12. Validate lead capture security controls and confirm no data exposure paths.

Exit criteria:
- `/privacy` updated and aligned with implementation
- data-sharing language present and clear

---

## TIER 4 — Structural and content risk

13. Add guardrails against copyrighted copied listing content without transformation/attribution.
14. Add owner-request process for listing removal/updates.
15. Audit branding claims for misleading authority/scale signals.

Exit criteria:
- process documented and linked where appropriate
- no misleading authority language in core marketing pages

---

## TIER 5 — Monetization alignment

16. Define monetization model in Terms before any new charges.
17. Avoid commissions/payment facilitation unless explicitly moving into regulated intermediary scope.

Exit criteria:
- Terms and pricing flows are legally consistent

---

## TIER 6 — Cleanup + operational validation

18. Fix broken/incomplete routes.
19. Ensure footer links to Terms + Privacy are present and accessible on all pages.
20. Run full first-time user journey test (discovery → listing → inquiry) and log issues.

Exit criteria:
- route crawl has no critical dead ends
- legal links are globally present
- journey QA checklist completed

---

## Suggested execution order

- Phase A: Tier 1 + Tier 3
- Phase B: Tier 2 + Tier 4
- Phase C: Tier 5 + Tier 6

No monetization expansion until Tier 1 + Tier 3 are complete.
