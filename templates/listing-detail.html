A daylight room for quiet, considered work.

Cyc & Co. is a 2,400 sqft daylight photo studio built around a seamless 18 ft white cyc wall and a 14 ft ceiling. North-facing clerestory windows pull soft, even light across the floor from morning to mid-afternoon; full blackout drops it to zero in seconds.

Tucked into a converted print shop on SE Division, it's a five-minute walk from Clinton/SE 12th and surrounded by the kind of bakeries and bars that make a long shoot day feel less like a chore. Designed for editorial, lookbook, e-commerce, and small-crew video.
